Trout Tacos

Description

These trout tacos take advantage of trout's mild, delicate flavor. With a semi-homemade slaw, crema, lime, and guacamole, these fish tacos are really tasty.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • purple cabbage
  • green onions
  • coleslaw mix
  • mexican crema
  • lime wedges
  • trout filets
  • guacamole

Steps

  1. preheat oven
  2. bake tortillas
  3. dip fish pieces
  4. heat oil
  5. use corn tortillas
